About

Minister of the Gospel,
Sustained by the Grace of God.

Gospel Advance exists for one reason: the lost need the gospel, God’s people need the truth, and Christ deserves all the glory. That’s it. That’s the mission.

We live in the most connected yet most hopeless generation — drowning in anxiety, depression, and distraction. Whether from a pulpit, on the streets, or through a screen — the aim is always the same. Bring the hope of Christ to a generation that desperately needs it. Faithfully proclaim the word of God wherever He opens the door. Just Christ-dependent, Christ-exalting ministry for every generation.

“His preaching was a continuation of his prayers. In both, he spoke from within the veil, his hand on the mercy seat and his eye fixed on the things invisible.”
— Robert Murray M’Cheyne
The Gospel

The message that changes everything.

Not a self-help program. Not advice. An announcement: God has done for you what you could never do for yourself.

01

God Created

We were made in God’s image, designed for relationship with Him and to reflect His glory.

Genesis 1:27
02

We Rebelled

Every person has sinned and fallen short. Our rebellion separated us from a holy God.

Romans 3:23
03

Christ Redeemed

Jesus lived perfectly, died for our sin, and rose again victorious over death.

Romans 5:8
04

We Respond

Repent and believe. Christianity doesn’t start with a feeling. It starts with an empty tomb.
